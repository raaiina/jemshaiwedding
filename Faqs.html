Can I bring someone else to your wedding with me? Unfortunately, no. We hope you understand that we can no longer accommodate those who are not on our guest list due to limited seats. Each seat has a name assigned.
Gift guide As love is what this day is all about, We require only your presence but if you were thinking of giving a gift, we would greatly appreciate a gift of cash to help save for our future.
What time should we arrive? The actual ceremony is at 4;00 PM. However, we recommend you getting here 30 minutes prior to the ceremony to ensure a smooth timeline or if you're going to the reception directly, please be there at exactly 6PM. Kindly consider the traffic and travel time.
Can we seat anywhere in the reception? NO. It took us a lot of effort and discussion to finish the seating arrangement which is planned for everyone's convenience & network preference.
When is the appropriate time to leave? I took us sometime to plan a wedding that everyone will hopefully enjoy. We aim to end the program around 10PM. Please dont eat and run.
Why RSVP is Important? It is important for us to identify the guest who are attending or not. Your RSVP also helps us to determine the final head count for food serving and seating arrangement.
What should we wear? If you are part of our principle sponsors we require you to wear formal attire for our guest, We encourage you to wear semi-formal attire.
